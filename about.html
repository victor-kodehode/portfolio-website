About Me

Hey! My name's Victor. I'm a participant on the IT AMO course Kodehode directed by Jobloop where I learn to become a full stack web developer. During my time here I've gotten good at several languages such as Python, JavaScript, HTML and CSS. Before the course I taught myself C, which made learning the other languages much easier. At this course I've shown, among other things, efficiency, ability to build and follow a routine, understand and tackle a project in steps and to meet deadlines.

I have a background in mathematics, physics and prior knowledge of programming in C. Because of this, logic and problem solving has become second nature to me, and this carries over to programming.
